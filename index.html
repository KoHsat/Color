What is Color Therpay?

Color therpy, also known as chromotherapy, is a form of therapy that use color and light to treat certain mental and physical health condtions. We can trace this form of therapy back to the ancient egyptians. They made use of sun-filled rooms with colored glasses for therapeutic purposes.
